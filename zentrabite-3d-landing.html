How it works

From first order to loyal regular.

ZentraBite connects every touchpoint so nothing falls through the cracks.

01
Customer places an order
Via your storefront, POS, or a third-party aggregator. Every order lands in the same board, in real time — no manual re-entry.

Kitchen screen, driver dispatch, stock deduction, and a customer SMS update are all triggered automatically the moment status changes.
03

Points credited, tier unlocked, win-back campaign queued if they go quiet. ZentraBite handles the follow-up so you don't have to.
